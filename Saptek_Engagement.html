Hosting — Why WordPress-Specific Matters

There are two categories of hosting: general-purpose servers (GoDaddy, Hostinger, Bluehost) and WordPress-specific managed hosting. For a business website representing SAPTEK to US enterprise clients, the difference is meaningful — and worth understanding before you make the call.

General hosting gives you server space and leaves the rest to you. WordPress-specific managed hosting is pre-configured, pre-optimised, and actively maintained for WordPress — automatic core updates, built-in staging environments, daily backups, and a support team that actually knows the platform. When something breaks at 11pm before a big client meeting, that distinction matters.
